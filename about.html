Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our programs, teaching methods, and enrollment process.

What grade levels do you serve?

We serve students from kindergarten through 12th grade. Our programs are designed to support learners at every stage, from building foundational skills to preparing for college-level work.

How do you personalize learning for each student?

We begin with a comprehensive assessment to understand each student's strengths, weaknesses, and learning style. Based on this, we create a customized learning plan and match students with teachers who specialize in their areas of need.

What qualifications do your teachers have?

All our teachers hold at least a bachelor's degree in their subject area, with many holding master's degrees or PhDs. They also undergo rigorous training in our teaching methodologies and receive ongoing professional development.

How do you track student progress?

We use a comprehensive progress tracking system that includes regular assessments, assignment completion rates, and skill mastery indicators. Parents receive detailed progress reports and can access real-time updates through our parent portal.

What is your success rate?

95% of our students show significant improvement in their grades within the first three months. Our college-bound students have a 98% acceptance rate at their top-choice schools, with many receiving academic scholarships.
